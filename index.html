The Praxis Program realigns graduate methodological training with the needs of the humanities in the digital age.

Our goal is to produce knowledge workers well-equipped for new faculty positions or alternative academic careers. Praxis Program students are scholars who are as comfortable writing code as they are managing teams and budgets.
